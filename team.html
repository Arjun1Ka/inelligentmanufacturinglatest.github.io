Isher Singh

Mechanical Engineering Professional

Isher Singh is a skilled professional with a Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Drexel University. During his master's program, he served as a Research Assistant under the guidance of Professor Richard Y. Chiou, where he notably designed the wind turbine laboratory on a virtual platform. Currently, Isher is employed as an Automotive Applications Engineer, where he specializes in designing automotive parts for vehicles, showcasing his expertise and contributions to the field of mechanical engineering and automotive design.

Ngozi Enwereji

Ngozi Enwereji is a diligent undergraduate student specializing in Engineering Technology with a focus on Robotics and Automation. In the 2022-2023 academic year, Ngozi made significant contributions by assisting in the refinement of learning manuals for RobotStudio software, enhancing the educational tools available to students and educators in this advanced field. Progressing into the 2023-2024 school year, Ngozi's role expanded to include the intricate task of digitally capturing a 120 ABB robot within a virtual environment. This endeavor highlights Ngozi's sophisticated technical skills and his innovative approach to the integration of robotics with virtual reality. Additionally, he undertook the meticulous work of testing the circular accuracy of actual robots, further evidencing his comprehensive approach to both the theoretical and practical aspects of robotics and automation.
